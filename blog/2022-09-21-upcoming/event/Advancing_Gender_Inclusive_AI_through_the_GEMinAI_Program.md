[CREATE SE4AI](https://se4ai.org/) and Applied AI Institute present a workshop at the 35th IEEE International Conference on Collaborative Advances in Software and Computing (CASCON 2025) on [Advancing Gender-Inclusive AI through the GEMinAI Program](https://conf.researchr.org/home/cascon-2025/geminai). Using the Gender Equity Mentoring in AI (GEMinAI) program as a successful use case, this workshop explores best practices and novel approaches to support and promote gender equality in AI. A panel discussion featuring experts from industry and academia, along with opportunities for networking, rich discussions and interactions will be offered. More details here: 
[https://conf.researchr.org/home/cascon-2025/geminai#About](https://conf.researchr.org/home/cascon-2025/geminai#About)
