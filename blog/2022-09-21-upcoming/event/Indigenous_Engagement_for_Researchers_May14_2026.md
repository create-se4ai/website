Discover how Indigenous communities are shaping and transforming research practices, and why intentional, relationship-based engagement is essential to impactful research. 
Through a grounded framework and guided reflection, you'll gain concrete tools and best practices for working with Indigenous stakeholders in ethical and meaningful ways.

This is a 2-hour, in-person workshop at Concordia. Participation will partially fulfill the requirements of CREATE SE4AI Professional Development module E.

Join us on Thursday, May 14 from 10 a.m. to noon at the SHIFT Centre for Social Transformation (LB-145).  Advance registration is required and spots are limited. 
For more information and to register: [https://www.concordia.ca/cuevents/offices/provost/oce/2026/05/14/indigenous-engagement-researchers.html?c=/about/shift/eventss](https://www.concordia.ca/cuevents/offices/provost/oce/2026/05/14/indigenous-engagement-researchers.html?c=/about/shift/events)
